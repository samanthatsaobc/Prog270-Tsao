Frodo in The Return of the King

After being rescued by Sam from the orcs, the ring is returned to Frodo. The pair finally reach Mount Doom, though they are followed by Gollum yet again. Gollum attacks but is beaten back by Frodo. However, Frodo no longer has a desire to destroy the ring and instead puts it on, thus becoming invisible.
Gollum, who had been held back by Sam, pushes past him and attacks Frodo, biting off his finger. Elated that he finally regained his "Precious", Gollum, dancing around carelessly, loses his balance and falls down into the lava along with the ring. The ring is destroyed, and consequently Sauron is as well.
