Frodo Baggins

Frodo Baggins is the nephew of Bilbo Baggins, the hobbit who originally obtained the ring from Gollum.
The ring then goes into Frodo's possession, and Frodo, along with his friend Sam, must take the ring to Mount Doom, which is in the heart of Mordor, to destroy it once and for all.
