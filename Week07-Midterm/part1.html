Frodo in The Fellowship of the Ring

In The Fellowship of the Ring, Frodo first obtains the ring after Bilbo goes to Rivendell. After 17 years, Gandalf comes to Bag End to warn Frodo of the danger of keeping the ring. Frodo, along with fellow hobbits Sam, Merry, and Pippin, head to Rivendell. On the way there, however, they are attacked by Ringwraiths and Frodo is stabbed in the shoulder by one of their attackers' blades.
They barely make it to Rivendell in time, for if they had taken any longer then Frodo would have turned into a Wraith. At Rivendell they form a fellowship to take the ring to Mount Doom, which consists of Frodo, Sam, Merry, Pippin, Gandalf, Aragorn, Legolas, Gimli, and Boromir.
On their journey, Gandalf dies battling a Balrog, and Boromir is killed while defending Merry and Pippin from orcs. At the end of this book the fellowship is broken. Frodo leaves the fellowship intending to travel alone, but Sam joins him.
