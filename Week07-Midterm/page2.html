Frodo in The Two Towers

In The Two Towers, Frodo and Sam continue their journey together. They run into Gollum and though Sam does not trust him, Frodo agrees to let Gollum be their guide.
Gollum promises the two hobbits that he will show them a secret way to get into Mordor. They go up the Endless Stair, Frodo and Sam unaware that Gollum is leading them into a trap. They then enter Shelob's lair, and while they manage to escape the giant spider manages to find them and she stings Frodo, rendering him unconscious.
Sam, after unsuccessfully trying to wake Frodo, thinks that he is dead and takes the ring. Soon after, several orcs find Frodo and realize that he is not dead, so they decide to take him back to their tower for questioning.
