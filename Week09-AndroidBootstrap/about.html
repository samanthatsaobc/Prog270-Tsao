Memo

This is a memo app, where you can write and then save notes. As you type, the app will display some words just above the keyboard, based on what it thinks you are trying to type. For example, in this screenshot I was typing the word "memo", and it displayed the words "memo," "memory," "memorandum," and so on. For more word options, you can click on the downward pointing arrow.
