Android Tablets

On this page you will find information about my experiences with an Asus Memo Pad (ASUS ME173X).

About this Tablet:

Memory 11.82 GB
Processor i.MX
Screen Size 496 x 794
Android version 4.2.1
Serial Number D60KCY046737

Android Customizations. Feature 01.

This page was added to the Android Settings by Asus, the company that makes this tablet. Here you can tweak various options, such as screenshots, gamer options, power options, scrolling options, and Asus specific hardware such as the trans cover. To see a full sized image, right click the image and choose View Image or Open image in new tab.

Android Settings Page. Feature 02.

This is the general settings page for the Android tablet. Here you can change certain settings by turning the Wi-Fi or Bluetooth on and off. Additionally, it links to other settings pages for sound, display, storage, battery, apps, power management, and ASUS customized settings, which you can see in the previous image. Also, there are personal settings including location access, security, language and input, and reset.

The Home Screen.
Feature 03.

This is the home screen, which you will see after you turn the tablet on. You can access this screen any time by tapping the house icon in the center of the bottom row of the screen. It currently is set to the default wallpaper, which is a nice picture of a blue sky and clouds. If you don't like the wallpaper or get tired of it, you can easily change it by going to the display settings. On the home screen you can see the current time and date, the weather in your current location, and the temperature. You can also do a google search by typing your search keywords in the box near the top of the home screen. Finally, you can see a list of all your apps by tapping the circle just above the house icon.
